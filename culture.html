Mentor First Initiative

The Cnit Lab is a proud supporter of the the Mentor First initiative. We believe that great science happens when everyone is supported, challenged, and encouraged to grow.

Our Lab Values

  • Growth Mindset: Mistakes are part of learning. We support each other in taking risks and growing from challenges.
  • Creativity: We encourage curiosity, bold ideas, and playful experimentation. There are no bad questions!
  • Respect: We treat each other with kindness and respect. Everyone's voice matters, and we listen to each other.
  • Integrity: We value honesty and transparency in our work.
  • Diversity & Inclusion: We celebrate different backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences. Everyone is welcome and respected.
  • Collaboration: Science is a team effort! We value open communication, sharing ideas, and helping each other succeed.
