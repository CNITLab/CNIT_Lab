Open Positions

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our lab. If you are interested in any of the positions below, please contact us.

  • Postdoctoral Researcher

    We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated postdoctoral researcher to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in chemistry, materials science, or a related field, and a passion for creative research.

    Express Interest

  • Graduate Research Assistant

    Interested in joining the CNIT Lab as a graduate student? Applications are handled through the ISTA Graduate School. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are passionate about research at the interface of chemistry, physics, and biology.

    If you are excited about our work, mention your interest in the CNIT Lab in your application and feel free to reach out with questions!

    Express Interest

  • Undergraduate Intern

    We offer internship opportunities and thesis projects for undergraduate students who are interested in gaining hands-on research experience. Interns will work closely with lab members on various projects.

    Express Interest

Postdoctoral Fellows

We are happy to support outstanding postdoctoral candidates in applying for fellowships and external funding. If you are interested in joining the lab as a postdoc at ISTA, please contact Friedrich Stricker with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

We are enthusiastic about supporting applications for relevant postdoctoral fellowships and are open to creative, interdisciplinary proposals!
